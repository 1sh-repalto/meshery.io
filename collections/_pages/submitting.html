Want to submit a pattern for the MeshMap Catalog? Here's how

Submit your MeshMap Designs to the Catalog

  1. Click the configuration button on left navigation bar. This reveals a dropdown.
  2. Find "Design" Button
  3. Find your design which you named in the design process.
  4. Fill out the required form including design details requested. If you do not complete this form, your design might be refused.
  5. Click the Publish button. Your design will be reviewed and published upon administrator specifications.
