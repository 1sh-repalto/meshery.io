Want to submit a pattern for the MeshMap Catalog? Here's how

Submit your MeshMap Designs to the Catalog

  1. Click the "Configuration" button on left navigation bar.
    ---This reveals a dropdown with 3 choices.

  2. You will see button with a tooltip called "Design".
    --Click "Design"

  3. Find your design, select it, and click "Publish" button within card containing your design."

  4. Fill out the required form.
    Include design details requested.
    ---If you do not complete this form, your design might be refused.

  5. Click the Publish button. Your design will be reviewed and published pending administrator review and specifications.
